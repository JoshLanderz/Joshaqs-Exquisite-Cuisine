About

Located south east of St. Micahel, JoShaq's Exquisite Cuisine is locally known for their refined but devine products such as custom made cakes and cupcakes, sweet and savory deserts (including pastries), pizza and catering to events whilst thriving to go above and beyond to exceed customers' expectations.
